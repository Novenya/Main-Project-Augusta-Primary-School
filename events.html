Events

Events allow students to learn many essential life skills.

New Year

These New Years Activities for Kids are great for both the classroom and home. They are so much fun.

Vesak Festival

Decorating class rooms, Making vesak lanterns, Lighting oil lamps are the things that students have to do in the vesak festival.

Poson Festival

Augusta primary school organized Sil Samadana Programme with this special day. The event witnessed a good participation.

Asela Festival

In this festival we organize the dansal and asala perahara for students.

Childern's Day

In that day they have to decorate their classes , play the game events , dance and sing songs.

Christmas Day

We organize the Christmas carols, Christmas card decor competition and enjoy with each other.
