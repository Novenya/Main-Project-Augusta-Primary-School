Welcome to AUGUSTA Primary International school

Augusta International School is a very well-regarded, high achieving and popular school located across 10 major cities in Sri Lanka. It is a fully-fledged K12 co-educational school network with English as the primary medium of instruction in teaching and learning.
At Augusta , we provide a foundation for life in a happy, high achieving school, where all individuals have high expectations of themselves, respect each other and strive to excel. The school offers a wide range of experiences based on a progressive curriculum, extra-curricular activities and involvement with the wider community.


At A Glance.

The idea of Augusta as an affordable fee levying private school network to teach the government syllabus in English Medium. A holistic approach to education with expert tutelage, creative freedom and inspiring innovation is the key to molding our students from core to advanced knowledge preserving the cultural and traditional values. We encourage an interactive student teacher classroom environment to fuel the student's curiosity and enhance self-discovery. We also firmly believe in inculcating intellectual awareness, cultural diversity and a sense of responsibility towards the community, in our student's mind.

OUR VALUES

We value inquiry and intellectual growth of all students and staff, recognising their different learning needs, encouraging them to be creative, engaged and lifelong learners

LIFE-LONG LEARNING

We are a community that strives to learn, to be intellectually curious, and to reflect upon what we have learned.

CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENTS

Excellence is more than a state of being; it demands an ethos of continuous self-examination and reflection focused on improvement.

STANDARDS OF CONDUCT

Our staff will act as role models and furthermore will instill respect, integrity and self-discipline in our students.

EXCELLENCE IN TEACHING

Best practices in teaching are utilized to develop the skills necessary for success in university, workplace and beyond.

RESPECT FOR DIVERSE PERSPECTIVES

We create a climate that encourages respect for a variety of ethnic groups, languages,opinions & cultures.

A COMMITMENT TO THE LOCAL AND GLOBAL COMMUNITY

Students develop social responsibility not as a burden or obligation, but as a vehicle for interaction and nation building.

WORKING BOTH INDEPENDENTLY AND COLLABORATIVELY

We learn to work independently by planning responsibly, managing our time, and being accountable for our actions.

HIGH MEASURABLE ACADEMIC STANDARDS

We offer a program of studies that enable our students to exceed national standards and be on par with the best in the world..
